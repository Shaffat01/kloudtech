Cloud vs. On-Premise: Which fits your business?

Cloud VoIP

Best for businesses that want flexibility, no capital expenses, and easy scalability.

  • No upfront hardware cost
  • Monthly subscription model
  • Automatic updates & features
  • Built-in redundancy
  • Quick deployment (days, not weeks)

On-Premise PBX

Best for large properties that need full control, have IT staff, or strict data policies.

  • One-time hardware investment
  • Complete data ownership
  • No monthly per-user fees
  • Customizable features
  • Works during internet outages

Multi-site integration for chains and franchises

Connect all your locations with unified dial plans. Transfer calls between sites seamlessly, share a single auto attendant, and centralize voicemail management.
