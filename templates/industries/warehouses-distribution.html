Industrial-grade hardware for demanding environments

Warehouses present unique challenges: metal racking, moving equipment, temperature variations, and dust. We specify hardware designed for these conditions.

  • Ruggedized Design: Extended temperature ratings for non-climate controlled spaces.
  • Industrial Ratings: Dust and vibration resistant enclosures (IP65/IP67).
  • Power Protection: Surge protection and redundant power for core network switches.
  • High-Gain Antennas: Specialized WiFi antennas to penetrate high-density racking.

Built for the floor

We don't use consumer gear. Every component is selected for durability and 24/7 reliability in industrial settings.
